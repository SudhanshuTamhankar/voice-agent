06 · The why

Built by someone who was once you.

I’m a first-generation college graduate who finished an MBA from IIM Bangalore, earned a 100% scholarship there, and secured an international offer of ₹85+ LPA CTC. Over 7+ years in education and mentoring 500+ students, I’ve seen that representation without preparation creates new barriers—many reach top colleges, few thrive.

Research shows students from marginalized backgrounds face higher dropout risks and lower placement outcomes. Not because of a lack of capability, but a lack in readiness.

Clymber exists to change that—helping students not just get in, but rise, by building the skills, mindset, and community to compete and belong.

08 · Frequently asked

Common questions, plainly answered.

Quick answers to what aspirants ask most before signing up. If your question isn't here, the readiness report is the fastest way to get a personalised answer.

What is Clymber?

Clymber is a personalized CAT preparation platform for MBA aspirants in India. It combines a diagnostic readiness report, structured practice across VARC, DILR, and QA, full-length CAT mock tests, and 1:1 mentorship from IIM alumni — so you train against the specific gaps the exam will exploit, not a generic syllabus.

Who is Clymber for?

Clymber is built for CAT aspirants who want a structured, mentor-led path to a top IIM — first-time test-takers, working professionals balancing prep with a job, and second-attempt students who need a sharper diagnosis of what's blocking their percentile.

How does Clymber's mentorship work?

Every Mentor Pro student is paired with an IIM alumnus for weekly 1-on-1 sessions covering doubt resolution, study plan adjustments, mock test analysis, and MBA interview preparation. Foundation and Self-Study plans include a smaller number of free consultation calls.

What is the readiness report?

The readiness report is a free 20-minute diagnostic. It produces a sectional baseline across VARC, DILR, and QA, a topic-level weak-spot map, and a realistic CAT target range — so you start your prep with a clear picture of where you are and what to fix first.

What is the difference between the Foundation, Self-Study, and Mentor Pro plans?

Foundation (Free) gives you the readiness report, daily reading practice, DILR foundation exercises, QA readiness check, vocabulary vault, and one free mentor call. Self-Study (₹3,999) adds paid video lectures, the full QA video course, sectional tests, and two free mentor calls. Mentor Pro (₹9,999) is the complete program: paid video lectures, QA videos, 25 full-length mocks, 300+ QA practice tests, weekly 1-on-1 mentor sessions, and a peer community.

Are Clymber's mentors actually IIM alumni?

Yes. Every Clymber mentor is an IIM alumnus or has equivalent top-tier MBA credentials. Mentor profiles, alma mater, and work background are shown before you book.

Can I prepare for CAT in 3 months with Clymber?

A 3-month plan is possible if you can commit consistent daily study time and start from a moderate baseline. The Clymber readiness report will tell you whether a 3-month timeline is realistic for your current preparedness — and what mock targets to hit weekly to stay on track.
